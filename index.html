A Letter for You 💌

My love, I just want to thank God for bringing you into my life.

I don’t even know how I started loving you this much… but now, you are my happiness. Even the smallest things you do mean so much to me.

I know sometimes I argue with you over unnecessary things and don’t behave the way I should. Sometimes, all I really want is your attention, but I don’t express it in the right way.

I will work on myself to improve my mistakes and become better for you. I truly want us to grow together, understand each other with love, and walk in the right path with God guiding us always.

““Be completely humble and gentle; be patient, bearing with one another in love. Make every effort to keep the unity of the Spirit through the bond of peace.””

Ephesians 4:2–3 ✨
